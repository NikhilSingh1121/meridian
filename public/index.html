The workflow

From ticker to defensible research.

Every stage of institutional research — extraction, computation, valuation, forensics, review — runs in sequence. Nothing is skipped, and every claim keeps its citation.

1

Open a ticker

Type any listed company — a persistent research workspace assembles itself from live filings in under ninety seconds.

2

Read the market first

Indices, rates, currencies, commodities and breadth on one dashboard — with a cap-weighted sector heatmap for the rotation read.

3

Filings become numbers

Reports and transcripts are extracted and standardized; every ratio is computed deterministically — never invented.

4

Chart it in real time

A full candlestick workspace on live data — fifteen-plus technical indicators, overlays and pattern intelligence across your portfolio.

5

Weigh the news

Headline flow is scored into a live sentiment gauge — company, industry or market-wide — so the news effect is measured, not guessed.

6

Value it three ways

A full FCFF working with the WACC build and sensitivity grid, cross-checked against comparables and sum-of-the-parts.

7

Run forensics before trust

Accruals, promoter pledges, related-party transactions and audit qualifications screened — each flag cited, never asserted.

8

Let an adversary read it

A bear-case agent files objections and a verifier checks every claim against its source before anything ships.

9

Ship the report

Cover page, numbered exhibits, the full DCF working — typeset to institutional standard and print-ready.

10

Keep compounding

A learning center that explains every concept in plain language, plus professional calculators — SIP to ESOP — with the math shown.
